Thoughtful essentials for precious little ones

Precious Koala was born in Melbourne out of a simple belief: the products that touch our babies should be safe, gentle and kind to the planet they'll grow up on.

We started with the everyday challenge every feeding parent knows: storing breast milk safely. So we designed a storage bag we'd trust ourselves: pre-sterilised, leak-proof, with a smart temperature indicator and a self-standing base built for the deep freezer.

Every detail is considered, and every bag is made from recyclable material. Because caring for your baby and caring for the world shouldn't be a trade-off.
