Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using our breast milk storage bags.

Home / FAQs

What makes Precious Koala storage bags special?
  • Recyclable, eco-friendly material
  • Gamma-ray pre-sterilised
  • Self-standing bag
  • Smart temperature indicator
  • Tamper-evident safety seal
  • Double zip lock, resealable and leak-proof
How do I use the storage bags?
  1. Prepare your area by sterilising all bottles, tools and surfaces.
  2. Wash your hands thoroughly.
  3. To open the bag, remove the perforated tab and open the dual closure.
  4. Pour breast milk into the bag and reseal for storage.
  5. Place the bag in the freezer.
  6. To defrost, place the bag in gentle warm water and remove it as soon as it reaches the appropriate temperature.
  7. Pour the milk into the feeding bottle.
How does the smart temperature indicator work?

The indicator changes colour so you can tell the milk's temperature at a glance:

Purple: Too cold (below 36°C) Blue: Safe to serve (36-40°C) White: Too hot (above 40°C)
Are there any warnings I should know?
  • Do not microwave or boil.
  • Never reuse breast milk bags. Reused bags will no longer be sterile and may be potentially harmful for the baby.
  • Once defrosted, breast milk should be used immediately.
Can I reuse a bag?

No. Each bag is single-use. Reused bags are no longer sterile and could be harmful to your baby. Used bags can, however, be washed, dried and then disposed of for recycling.

How do I dispose of used bags?

Used bags can be washed, dried and then disposed of for recycling, keeping things kind to the planet.

What capacity are the bags?

Each bag holds up to 220ml, with clear measurement markings. They come in 30, 60 and 90-bag packs.
